# Code of conduct

## Our standards

Be direct, kind, and specific. Good collaboration here looks like clear bug
reports, focused patches, and feedback that helps someone improve the work
without making them feel small.

Examples of welcome behavior:

- Giving concrete reproduction steps.
- Explaining tradeoffs without personal attacks.
- Keeping disagreement about the code, docs, or process.
- Respecting boundaries and private contact details.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, or sustained personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or attention.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Dismissing someone based on identity, background, or experience level.
- Derailing issues or pull requests after being asked to stop.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit, hide, or remove comments and may block people from the
project when behavior makes collaboration unsafe or unworkable.

If you need to report conduct concerns, email `lila.m.brooks@gmail.com`.
Include links, screenshots, or dates when they help explain what happened.
